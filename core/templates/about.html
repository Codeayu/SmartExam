About SmartExam

Our Story

SmartExam was born from a simple observation: students spend countless hours preparing for exams, often unsure if they're focusing on the right topics or questions.

Founded in 2024 by a team of AI specialists and educational experts, our mission is to make exam preparation more efficient, personalized, and accessible for students worldwide.

By analyzing course syllabi and leveraging the latest in artificial intelligence, SmartExam generates highly relevant practice questions that help students prepare strategically for their exams.
